Zero-Emission & Human-Centric Mobility

Prioritizing pedestrian, bicycle, and electric public transit networks dramatically reduces congestion and air pollution.

The 15-Minute City Concept

Designing neighborhoods where all essential services (work, shopping, school, healthcare) are accessible within a 15-minute walk or bike ride, reducing the need for cars entirely.

  • Dedicated, separated bike and pedestrian paths.
  • Enhanced public safety through better street lighting and visibility.
  • Redeveloping vehicle lanes into shared public spaces.

Integrated Public Transit & EV Adoption

The seamless integration of electric buses, trams, and rail, linked by smart ticketing systems and real-time data, makes public transport the most convenient choice.

  • Ubiquitous, fast charging infrastructure for shared and private EVs.
  • On-demand electric micro-mobility (scooters/bikes) programs.
  • Congestion pricing to discourage single-occupancy fossil-fuel vehicles.
