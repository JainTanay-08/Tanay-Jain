Harnessing 100% Renewable Energy

The energy transition is at the heart of the sustainable city model, relying on diverse and smart renewable sources.

Solar Photovoltaics (PV)

The most accessible urban renewable. Integration into building facades, rooftops, and parking lot canopies maximizes decentralized power generation.

Urban Wind Energy

Small-scale, vertical-axis wind turbines are designed for urban environments, utilizing localized air currents to supplement solar power generation.

Intelligent Power Grids

Smart grids use bidirectional communication to balance intermittent renewable generation with demand, integrating battery storage and EV charging networks seamlessly.

The Challenge: Energy Storage

Intermittency is overcome through utility-scale battery storage and Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) technology, turning electric cars into distributed energy resources when parked.
